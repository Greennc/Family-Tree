Who Killed Jeff Doby?

In 1952 Jeff's family recieved news that there had been an accident. Jeff was on his way home from fighting in World War II and at a stop in Bioloxi, Mississippi he became drunk. His family was told that in his drunkenness he was accidently run over by a train. He was dead at just 31 years old. However, when an autopsy was peformed on him, it was discovered that his body had bullets in it. His true cause of death was murder.
